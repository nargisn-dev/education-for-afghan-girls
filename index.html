The surest way to make the world more just, sustainable, and peaceful is to educate girls and empower women.

We envision a future where educated Afghan girls and women become the architects of change in their lives and help rebuild Afghan society from the confines of inequality, poverty, and limited opportunities. Through our platform, we foresee these young women becoming the leaders of their communities working towards prosperity, innovation, and social progress. We work together with our passionate volunteers and dedicated partners to create a pathway where education becomes a beacon of hope, empowerment, and transformation in Afghanistan.

The idea is to give them hope. To be a girl in Afghanistan today is hell. There’s absolutely no future. The idea for us is to say okay, you’re forbidden to go to school, you’re not forbidden to learn!
